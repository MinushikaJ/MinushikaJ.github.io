About Me

My research lies at the intersection of the Internet of Things (IoT) and machine learning, with a strong interest in intelligent systems and broader artificial intelligence (AI) applications. I focus on developing smart, connected solutions that can collect, analyze, and act on real-time data from distributed IoT devices. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, I aim to uncover patterns and make predictive insights that enhance system responsiveness and automation. My work contributes to building intelligent systems capable of adaptive behavior, optimized decision-making, and im- proved user experiences. I am particularly passionate about integrating AI-driven techniques into real-world IoT environments to solve practical challenges across healthcare, smart infrastructure, and industrial automation.
